The Flying Machines

HAWKER 390

The Hawker Beechcraft Premier 1A Model 390 is a remarkable aircraft that combines luxurious comfort, exceptional performance, and cutting-edge technology. It is a popular choice among discerning business travelers and aviation enthusiasts. This light business jet has earned a stellar reputation in the aviation industry. The Premier 1A boasts a sleek and aerodynamic design, exuding elegance and sophistication. Its compact size and agile performance make it an ideal choice for executives and entrepreneurs who value efficiency and flexibility in their travel arrangements. With its ability to access smaller airports and shorter runways, the Premier 1A provides unmatched convenience and accessibility.

DIAMOND DA62

The DA 62's spacious cabin accommodates up to seven passengers, the ergonomically designed seats ensure a pleasant and relaxing journey for all on board.. Additionally, the generous baggage compartment allows for convenient storage of personal belongings and equipment.

The Diamond DA 62 is a remarkable aircraft that combines elegance, efficiency, and advanced technology in a versatile and capable package. Its stylish design, spacious cabin and cutting-edge avionics make it a preferred choice for private owners and corporate operators.
